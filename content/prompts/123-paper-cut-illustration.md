> Transform this photo into a cute paper-cut style illustration. Faithfully preserve all subjects, their appearance, clothing, expressions, poses, and the background scenery exactly as shown in the photo. Apply soft pastel color palette, layered 3D paper craft effect, simple rounded facial features with rosy cheeks, stylized minimal background in layered paper design, soft lighting, smooth shadows, cozy and wholesome vibe, high detail, depth through stacked paper layers, whimsical and clean composition.
