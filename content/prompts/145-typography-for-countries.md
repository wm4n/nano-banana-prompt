### Prompt

> Create a premium 9:16 hyper-realistic travel-poster of {COUNTRY NAME} in the same style as a luxury editorial miniature-map diorama: show the country as a recognizable floating island map with dramatic cliff edges, crystal-clear water, soft clouds, and a pure white  background. Use a clean, elegant poster layout with large culturally appropriate typography for “{COUNTRY NAME}” at the top, using a font style inspired by the country’s identity, history, script, or visual culture, plus a short refined tagline. The map must be the hero: ultra-detailed terrain, mountains, forests, rivers, lakes, coastlines, farmland, deserts or snow where relevant, and each major region designed with its own architecture, colors, traditions, and landscape character. Highlight the capital city as a cinematic modern metropolis, and include famous cities, landmarks, monuments, temples, castles, mosques, churches, shrines, bridges, roads, railways, airports, boats, trains, cars, and tiny people for lively environmental storytelling. Add thin elegant callout labels for important cities and places. At the bottom, include clean infographic-style fact sections with icons for population, language, currency, tourism, culture, transport, exports, cuisine, and national symbols, plus optional small feature panels for landmarks, food, nature, cities, and heritage. Style it as an 8K luxury tourism poster—bright, polished, highly zoomable, cinematic, culturally rich, minimal, refined, and visually consistent with premium travel-editorial map posters.
