### Prompt

<img width="750" alt="Watercolor Anime-Style Illustration" src="/nano-banana-prompt/images/prompts/133-watercolor-anime-style-illustration-1.png" />

> A soft watercolor anime-style illustration of a young girl sitting on a low concrete ledge, centered in a 1:1 square composition. She has short, slightly messy brown hair blowing gently in the wind, big expressive eyes looking slightly upward with a calm, dreamy expression.
> She is wearing a loose white t-shirt, rolled-up blue jeans, and simple light-colored slip-on shoes. Her legs are crossed casually, and she is holding a small cup in both hands near her lap.
> Behind her, there are bright yellow sunflowers with loose painterly strokes blending into a warm, abstract background. The background is minimal and softly textured, with splashes of warm yellow, beige, and light orange tones, creating a peaceful, nostalgic atmosphere.
> Use delicate ink outlines combined with soft watercolor washes, subtle paint splatters, and a hand-painted sketch aesthetic. Lighting is warm and diffused, with gentle highlights and no harsh shadows.
> Composition: centered subject, full body visible, balanced spacing, square frame (1:1 aspect ratio).
> Style tags:
> 
> anime watercolor, Studio Ghibli inspired, soft illustration, hand-painted, pastel tones, warm aesthetic, dreamy mood, sketch + watercolor blend, high detail
> Negative prompt:
> 
> text, watermark, logo, extra limbs, distorted face, harsh shadows, photorealistic, 3D render, overly sharp lines
> Recommended settings (if needed):
> 
> Aspect ratio: 1:1
> 
> Quality: high
> 
> Stylization: medium-high
