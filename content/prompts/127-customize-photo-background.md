### Prompt

<img width="750" alt="Customize Photo Background" src="/nano-banana-prompt/images/prompts/127-customize-photo-background-1.png" />

> A candid, high-resolution photo of a smiling man in a cozy, artisanal cafe, holding a ceramic coffee cup. The man is seated on a rustic wooden bench with a matching wooden table, looking off to the side with a broad smile. He wears a dark grey marled knit sweater and a metal-and-leather watch. The cafe background is bustling with blurred figures of other patrons and shelves with various items and bottles, suggesting an active atmosphere. Natural light streams in from a large window on the right, which shows a view of a brick exterior and distant buildings. The window area has potted plants and string lights. A small potted succulent sits on the table near a notebook. A set of colorful (pastel and neon) doodle-style elements are integrated into the scene: a glowing light-yellow line outlines the man's figure; and various small, hand-drawn stars, dots, and a few abstract heart shapes in a cluster of light-pink, blue, purple, and green outlines float around his head and near the cup, creating a playful, creative effect. The overall lighting is warm and natural, combining ambient cafe light with window daylight. The focus is sharp on the man and his cup, with a shallow depth of field rendering the background cafe details in soft bokeh. The style is a blend of realistic photography and hand-drawn digital overlay.
