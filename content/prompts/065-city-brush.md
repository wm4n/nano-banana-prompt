### Prompt

> A brush, captured mid-motion, paints a refined curve from the lower left to the upper right.
> Within the brushstroke lives a miniature world of {CITY}, rendered as a highly detailed 3D bas-relief city sculpted from layered paint.
>
> Inside the stroke:
> Iconic landmarks, architecture, and spatial rhythms of {CITY}, arranged as a cohesive miniature with depth and subtle relief.
>
> Style:
> Impasto oil painting fused with academic poster design. Thick and thin paint layers create depth, with 3D forms emerging from the stroke.
> Color palette follows the official flag colors of the country associated with {CITY}, blended naturally.
>
> View & background:
> Minimalist top-down composition, pure white negative space, fine textured paper.
> 
> Typography:
> Title: “{CITY} METROPOLIS”
> Poetic subtitle and supporting serif copy, cleanly spaced.
>
> Composition rules:
> Brush tip ends cleanly at the upper right. All detail remains strictly inside the stroke.
