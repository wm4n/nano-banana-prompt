>

>
> Use a clean solid {BACKGROUND COLOR} background.
> At the top-center, display {TECH TOPIC} in large bold text, beneath it show a subtitle like {FROM YEAR – TO YEAR}, and place a minimal tech icon below.
>
> All text must automatically match the background contrast (white or black)
